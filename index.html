I made an infomercial for the concept of a save button. In addition to what was used in my audio assignment, I found stock footage of a man with various emotions, a cup spilling over, and a save button on a computer. I recorded the voiceover myself, along with the man crying. I found some stock music and a record scratch sound effect online.
